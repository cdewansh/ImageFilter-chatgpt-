1. Create an account:
    • Click on the Register link.
    • Fill in the required information to create your account.
  2. Log in:
    • Click on the Login link.
    • Enter your username and password.
  3. Upload a picture:
    • Navigate to the Home page.
    • Click on the "Select an Image to edit" button.
    • Choose an image file from your device and click "Submit."
  4. View edited images:
    • After uploading, you will be redirected to the dashboard.
    • Explore the edited images and have fun experimenting with different filters!
